AMENITIES

Stalls

Our 8 x 10 ft. stalls are padded and shavings are cleaned and replaced daily. The horses boarded in the stalls have daily turnout and they have an attached run-out in their stall. The stalls are equipped with automatic watering.

Paddocks

Our paddocks have covered padded run-ins. The size of the paddocks vary. Lighting and electric fencing surrounding all paddocks

Arenas

Two fully enclosed indoor arenas with training footing. One lighted outdoor arena with arena sand footing. Groomed daily. Heated viewing area, restroom facility.
